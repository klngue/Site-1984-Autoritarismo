Authoritarianism x 1984

This page will address the chosen subject referring to the book, that is, some events, complications and quotes, taken from 1984 and how they fit in the context of Authoritarian Regimes

Reduced cognitive ability

"As long as they don't become aware, they won't be authentic rebels, and until they rebel, they can't be made aware."

Power and Influence over Society

“Power is inflicting pain and humiliation. Power is tearing apart the human mind and then putting the pieces back together again, shaping them into whatever shape you want.”

Hierarchy and Social Inequality

Society in 1984 is divided into the Lower Party, the Outer Party and the Proletariat, showing itself as a stratified and unequal environment.

Breach of Privacy and Private Life

All individuals are watched by cameras, telescreens, wiretaps and even family members

manipulation of truth

“Who controls the past, controls the future; who controls the present, controls the past.”

Opposition persecution and extermination

In the novel, Winton and Julia are tortured at the Ministry of Love for opposing the Party. In order for the opposition to the Party, it is the conquest of a lover of the same.
